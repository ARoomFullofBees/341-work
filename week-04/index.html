Nostradamus Cherry Jelly

Photos of the process can be found at fxcuisine.com

published by Nostradamus in 1555

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 4 pounds of stemmed cherries.
    • If the Cherryes are not Rype, that they are Green even a Bit, they will be so Sour that they will cause Stupefaction to your Teeth.

    Also Needed:

  • 1 clean stick
  • 1 clean cloth
  • 1 copper pot
    • Pot can be of another metal if jelly isn't intended for clairvoyance purposes.
    • A candy thermometer is also recommended for non-alchemically correct version.
  • 1 Jelly Jar

Instructions

  1. Take Cherryes the most Beautiful & most Rype that can be found, in the quantity you wish, and remove the Foot.
  2. Take Sugar that is Nice & put it in coarse Powder & place in a Pan & it should have weight of two Pounds.
  3. Take Cherryes that you have removed the Foot, the Weight of Six or Seven Pounds, and Grate and Rub them Coarseley with Clean Hands.
    • This must be done with bare hands and not a utensil.
  4. Place in the Pan where the Sugar is and place on the Fire to Boil until Half, moving with a clean Stick.
  5. And when they have so Boiled you will Pour them through a Clean and Tight Cloth. [until] all has been well pressed and that only the Bones & Bark remains.
    • Keep away from giving it too much fire as it would go over or burn itself as it is better for the Jelly to be a little Green than too Cooked: because when Stored its Sugar makes it Dry.
    • When you will see that it is Cooked, that is a drop put on Marble, the drop remains tight and Round without Falling neither here nore there, or placed on a piece of tin or on a Knife or Glass the drop will be as Vermillion as a perfect Claret Wine.
  6. When it is Cooked you will put it in little Jars of Glass or Wood like you would Quince Jelly.
  7. And when your Jelly will be Colde and Tight, you will have a Mode and Type of Jelly that you can't have Better Jelly of Cherryes, nor more Excellent in Beauty and Goodness.

and if afterwards you look through the Sun's light you will find it as Beautiful as a Ruby. You have to show or give to some great Prince or high Lord when they will be angry with the Heat or otherwise altered they will find the Jam hearty & Delicious & Without Defects. And if you do it like I have put on Paper, undoubtedly you will do a Jelly in all Perfection.
